Crepes

Welcome to the ultimate guide for making crepes! These flat and crispy treats are a treat and the perfect delight to satisfy your sweet tooth. They are perfect for breakfast, lunch or a desert for dinner. Follow this simple recipe to create a delicious, crispy-on-the-outside, chewy-on-the-inside crepe that everyone will love. You can also top it off with anything sweet that youd like.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1.5 cups milk
  • 2 eggs
  • Pinch of salt
  • (Optional: 1 tbsp melted butter or sugar for sweet crepes)

Instructions

  1. Whisk all ingredients until smooth.
  2. Let the batter rest for 10-15 minutes (optional but improves texture).
  3. Heat a lightly greased non-stick pan over medium heat.
  4. Pour in a small amount of batter, swirl to coat thinly.
  5. Cook until edges lift, flip, and cook the other side briefly.
